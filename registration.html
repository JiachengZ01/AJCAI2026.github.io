Registration

Thank you for your interest in attending the Australasian Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AJCAI 2026). Below are the registration fees and rules for AJCAI 2026.

Each accepted paper must be covered by a separate registration — either a full/student registration, or the Additional Paper add-on within a full/student registration.

Conference Registration Entitlements

Full Registration & Student Registration
  • Full access to 27 November Workshops and Tutorials, and the Encore track
  • Full access to 28–30 November Main Conference, including:
    • 28 Nov PhD Forum
    • 28 Nov Responsible AI Symposium
    • 29 Nov Industry Day
    • 29 Nov AI Faculty Highlight
  • 27–30 November Conference Catering
  • All Social Functions, including the Welcome Reception (28 Nov) and Conference Dinner (29 Nov)
Single Day Registration
  • Access to that day's sessions and conference catering
  • No access to social functions included
  • Social function tickets available to purchase as add-ons at additional cost
