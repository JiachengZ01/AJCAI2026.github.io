Visas & Immigration

Some people including citizens of visa waiver countries can travel to New Zealand with an NZ Electronic Travel Authority (NZeTA) instead of a visa.

People travelling on a passport from some countries must apply for a Visitor Visa to visit New Zealand.

Visa information is available from Immigration New Zealand (INZ):
https://www.immigration.govt.nz/

Visitors from visa-waiver countries must request an Electronic Travel Authority (ETA) before coming to New Zealand. You may also have to pay an International Visitor Conservation and Tourism Levy (IVL). For more information on the ETA and IVL, visit Immigration New Zealand.

Visa Invitation Letter

Visa letters of invitation can be issued only to attendees who have registered for the conference and have paid for their registration. You could request your visa invitation letter during the registration process or by emailing AJCAI2026_registration@auckland.ac.nz after completing your conference registration.

The Conference cannot contact or intervene with any INZ on your behalf, so please register early and begin your visa application process as early as possible.
